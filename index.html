About Us

Finaax Capital Advisors Private Limited is an Energetic SEBI Registered Category – 1 Merchant Banker, engaged in providing a range of services including Strategic Fundraising, Corporate Finance, Corporate Advisory, Restructurings, and Valuations. We are committed to delivering high-quality financial advisory solutions.

Founded by seasoned professionals with over a decade of experience in Capital Markets and Corporate Finance, Finaax Capital brings deep domain expertise and industry insight. Our team comprises experienced Company Secretaries, Chartered Accountants, and MBAs, creating a dynamic blend of creativity and professionalism. This diverse and skilled talent pool significantly enhances the value and quality of services we deliver to our clients. At Finaax, we are driven by a commitment to excellence, integrity, and long-term client relationships.
