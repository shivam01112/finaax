About Us

At Shannon Advisors, we stand as a distinguished beacon of expertise and growth-focused consultancy, guided by seasoned professionals boasting rich experience in both financial services and core industry sectors. Our dynamic team comprises devoted experts, including Chartered Accountants, Lawyers, and Company Secretaries, all united in our unwavering commitment to excellence.

What sets Shannon apart is our pride in comprehending and addressing the unique needs of our clients. Moving beyond conventional consultancy, we proactively sculpt strategies to propel success. Our emphasis lies in optimizing resource utilization, offering specialized advice on fundraising, corporate restructuring, open offer and ensuring seamless asset coordination.
